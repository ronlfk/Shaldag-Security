Privacy Policy for Mobile Games and Applications

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Shaldag Security (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information in connection with all mobile games and applications published by us (the “Apps”). We are committed to protecting user privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Policy applies to all games and applications developed or published by Shaldag Security and made available on platforms such as Google Play, the Apple App Store, and other distribution platforms. By downloading or using our Apps, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

3. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

However, our Apps may collect limited non-personal and device-related information automatically through third-party SDKs integrated into the Apps.

4. Third-Party SDKs and Advertising

Our Apps use third-party services to support monetization, performance, and functionality. These services may automatically collect certain information as described below.

  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements within our Apps.

Google AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (may be used to estimate general location)
  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks, impressions)

This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google’s own Privacy Policy.

We do not have direct access to or control over the data collected by third-party SDKs.

5. How Information Is Used

Information collected via third-party SDKs may be used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying ads and measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving app stability and user experience

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under the applicable age of consent in the user’s jurisdiction).

Advertising shown to users may be configured to comply with applicable child protection laws (including COPPA), where required.

7. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party SDKs is protected by the security measures implemented by those providers.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data ourselves. Data retention practices of third-party services are governed by their own policies.

9. User Choices

Users may control ad personalization through their device settings, including resetting or limiting the use of advertising identifiers where supported by the operating system.

10. International Data Transfers

Third-party services used in our Apps may process data on servers located outside your country. Such transfers are handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
